BJP's contemplation for the victory of Gujarat elections for 2-days, many veteran leaders and Amit Shah will be present

  • May 15, 2022

BJP Meeting: As part of the preparations for the elections, BJP will conduct a Chintan Shivir from today. The two-day contemplation camp is being held in the presence of Union Home Minister Amit Shah.

Gujarat Election 2022

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) has gathered in the preparations for the assembly elections to be held in Gujarat this year. The party will conduct a Chintan Shivir from today as part of the preparations for the elections. The two-day contemplation camp is being held in the presence of Union Home Minister Amit Shah. Bhupendra Yadav, Union Minister Mansukh Mandaviya and Purushottam Rupala will also be present in the camp. In the meeting, the work of the government and the organization so far will be reviewed.

The roadmap for the upcoming assembly elections will be decided in the camp. In this, the challenges faced by the party in the near future will also be discussed. 40 prominent leaders will be present in the meeting. The election preparations of Congress and AAP will also be discussed in the meeting. Members of BJP core committee will be present in the meeting. Apart from this, members of the BJP Parliamentary Board will also be present in the camp.

In the two-day consultation meeting, the board corporation will be discussed after the elections are over soon. The names of board corporations can be discussed in the contemplation meeting. From the 1400 bio-data received for the corporation, the board can put the final seal. 98 The Chairman of the Board may prepare the Chairman and Directors of the Corporation.

The review report can be discussed in the meeting of the associate organization and the SC-ST Morcha. Issues related to the 2022 elections can be discussed in the report card on the current issues and performance of Gujarat.

BJP is running its rule over Gujarat for 27 years. Gujarat is already in election mode. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has made two visits to the state in the last few months. During these tours, PM Modi blew the election bugle in Gujarat.

PM Modi will make many more visits to Gujarat in the coming months. Gujarat has attracted not only state and central politicians but also people from abroad, including Prime Minister of the United Kingdom Boris Johnson and Prime Minister of Mauritius Pravind Kumar Jugnauth respectively. At the same time, apart from PM Modi, Union Home Minister Amit Shah has also visited Gujarat.
